680.26(B) Equipotential Bonding — Bonded Parts

Bonded part
Bonded & grounded (G)
#8 AWG copper bond

Bonding method — 680.26(B)(1) through (B)(7)

The parts above are bonded together using:

  • Solid copper conductor — insulated, covered, or bare — not smaller than 8 AWG, or
  • Rigid metal conduit of brass or other identified corrosion-resistant metal.

(G) = also required to be grounded (connected to the equipment grounding system), in addition to being bonded.

Source: National Electrical Code (NEC) § 680.26(B)
